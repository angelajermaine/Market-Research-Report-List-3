<p><p>Explosion Proof Temperature Switches are designed to operate safely in hazardous environments where there is a risk of explosion. There are two main types of market for these switches: Direct Mount and Remote Mount. Direct Mount switches are directly installed in the hazardous area, while Remote Mount switches are installed away from the hazardous area with a sensor probe extending into the dangerous location. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and preventing potential explosions in industrial settings where temperature control is crucial.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Combustion-Type Industrial Furnace</li><li>Electric-Type Industrial Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial furnaces can be categorized into two main types: combustion-type and electric-type. Combustion-type industrial furnaces use fossil fuels such as natural gas or oil to generate heat for processes like melting, forging, or heat treatment. On the other hand, electric-type industrial furnaces use electricity as the source of heat, offering a more precise and controllable heating process. Both types of furnaces are widely used in various industries such as metallurgy, ceramics, and foundries for their efficiency and reliability in heating materials to high temperatures.</p></p>

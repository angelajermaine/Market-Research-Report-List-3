<p><p>The Ultrasonic Flowmeter market is segmented into three main types: Spool Piece Ultrasonic Flowmeter, Insertion Ultrasonic Flowmeter, and Clamp-On Ultrasonic Flowmeter. </p><p>Spool Piece Ultrasonic Flowmeters are installed between two flanges in a pipeline, providing accurate flow measurement. </p><p>Insertion Ultrasonic Flowmeters are inserted into a pipe through a hole, making them suitable for larger pipe sizes.</p><p>Clamp-On Ultrasonic Flowmeters are non-invasive, clamped onto the outside of the pipe to measure flow, making them ideal for retrofitting existing systems without disrupting operations.</p></p>

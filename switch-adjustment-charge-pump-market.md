<p><p>Switch adjustment charge pump market can be divided into two main types based on their operation: AC-DC and DC-DC. AC-DC charge pumps convert alternating current (AC) input to direct current (DC) output, commonly used in power adapters and electronic devices that require a steady DC voltage. On the other hand, DC-DC charge pumps convert one DC voltage level to another, useful for mobile phones, laptops, and other battery-powered devices. Both types play a crucial role in efficiently managing power conversion in electronic systems.</p></p>

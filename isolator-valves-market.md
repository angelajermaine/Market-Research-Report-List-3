<p><p>Isolator valves can be divided into different types based on their design and function. The main types of isolator valves in the market include ball valves, butterfly valves, gate valves, globe valves, and other specialized valves. Ball valves operate by rotating a ball to control flow, while butterfly valves use a disc to regulate flow. Gate valves are designed to fully open or close, while globe valves control flow using a plug. Other market options include specialty valves for specific applications such as diaphragm valves and pinch valves.</p></p>

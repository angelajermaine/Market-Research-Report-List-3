<p><strong>The Beam Stacker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Beam Storage</li><li>Double Beam Storage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Beam stacker market can be categorized into single beam storage and double beam storage systems. Single beam storage systems have one beam per level and are suitable for storing lighter loads or small items. On the other hand, double beam storage systems have two beams per level, providing more stability and capacity to hold heavier loads. Double beam storage systems are ideal for storing bulkier items or larger quantities of goods. Both types of systems are commonly used in warehouses and distribution centers to optimize storage space and efficiency.</p></p>

<p><p>The Microneedle Flu Vaccine market offers different types of microneedles for administration, including Solid Microneedle, Hollow Microneedle, Coated Microneedle, and Dissolving Microneedle. Solid Microneedles have a solid structure that penetrates the skin to deliver the vaccine. Hollow Microneedles have a hollow center that allows for the vaccine to be injected directly into the skin. Coated Microneedles are coated with the vaccine, which is released upon insertion into the skin. Dissolving Microneedles dissolve in the skin, releasing the vaccine for absorption.</p></p>

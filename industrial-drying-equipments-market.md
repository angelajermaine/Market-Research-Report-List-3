<p><strong>The Industrial Drying Equipments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Dryer</li><li>Dispersion Dryers</li><li>Fluid Bed Dryers</li><li>Belt Dryers</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial drying equipment market consists of various types such as rotary dryers, dispersion dryers, fluid bed dryers, belt dryers, and others. Rotary dryers are cylindrical in shape and widely used for drying materials. Dispersion dryers use high-velocity air to disperse the material for efficient drying. Fluid bed dryers use hot air to fluidize and dry particles. Belt dryers pass materials through a heated belt for drying. Other types include freeze dryers, vacuum dryers, and spray dryers, each with unique drying processes.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Radiofluoroscopy System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Field</li><li>Midfield</li><li>High Field</li><li>Ultra-High Field</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiofluoroscopy systems are classified into four types based on the strength of the magnetic field they operate at: Low Field, Midfield, High Field, and Ultra-High Field. Low Field systems have a magnetic field strength below 0.5 Tesla, Midfield systems operate at 0.5-1.5 Tesla, High Field systems are in the range of 1.5-3 Tesla, and Ultra-High Field systems have a magnetic field strength above 3 Tesla. Each type offers varying levels of imaging capabilities and applications based on the strength of the magnetic field.</p></p>

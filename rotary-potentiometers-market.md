<p><p>A rotary potentiometer, also known as a rotary variable resistor, is an electro-mechanical device used to control the flow of electric current in a circuit. It consists of a resistive element and a sliding contact that moves along the element. By rotating the knob of the potentiometer, the resistance between the contact and the ends of the element can be adjusted, thereby controlling the output voltage or current of the circuit.</p><p>The Rotary Potentiometers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.10% during the forecast period.

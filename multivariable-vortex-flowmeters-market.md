<p><p>Multivariable vortex flowmeters can be categorized into two main market types: inline type and insertion type. Inline type vortex flowmeters are directly installed into pipelines and are suitable for continuous flow measurement. On the other hand, insertion type vortex flowmeters are designed to be inserted into existing pipelines without the need for cutting the pipe. Both types offer accurate measurement of flow rates and are commonly used in industries such as oil & gas, chemical, and water treatment.</p></p>

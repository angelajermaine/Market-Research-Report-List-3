<p><p>Multi-layering chip inductors are commonly categorized into two types: ceramic core and magnetic core. Ceramic core inductors are known for their high frequency performance, stability, and reliability, making them suitable for applications in RF circuits and filters. On the other hand, magnetic core inductors offer higher inductance values and lower DC resistance, making them more suitable for power supply and energy storage applications. Both types cater to different market segments based on their unique characteristics and performance capabilities.</p></p>

<p><p>Colonoscope Video Endoscopes are categorized into two main types: Rigid Video Endoscopes and Flexible Video Endoscopes. </p><p>Rigid Video Endoscopes are typically used for procedures that require a straight-line access to the target area, such as in colonoscopies. They are equipped with a rigid shaft and a camera at the tip to provide high-quality images.</p><p>Flexible Video Endoscopes, on the other hand, are more versatile and are able to maneuver through the body's curves and bends. They are commonly used in procedures where a flexible navigation is required, such as in gastroscopies.</p><p>Both types of endoscopes play a crucial role in modern medical procedures by allowing healthcare professionals to visualize and diagnose internal conditions effectively.</p></p>

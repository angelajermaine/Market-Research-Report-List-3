<p><p>Wearable Powered Exoskeleton market consists of two main types: Partial Body Exoskeleton and Full Body Exoskeleton. Partial Body Exoskeletons are designed to assist specific body parts, such as the upper or lower limbs, offering support and enhancing strength for tasks like lifting heavy objects. On the other hand, Full Body Exoskeletons cover the entire body and provide overall support for mobility and strength, benefiting users with mobility impairments or working in physically demanding environments. Both types aim to improve user performance and reduce physical strain.</p></p>

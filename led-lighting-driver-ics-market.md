<p><p>LED Lighting Driver ICs are available in two main types: SMD Type and Through Hole Type. SMD Type LEDs are surface-mount devices that are smaller in size and suitable for compact applications. On the other hand, Through Hole Type LEDs have longer leads that are inserted through holes in a circuit board for a secure connection. Both types of driver ICs offer different advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application, such as size constraints or ease of installation.</p></p>

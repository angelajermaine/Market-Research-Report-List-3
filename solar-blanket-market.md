Oval solar blankets are designed specifically for oval or kidney-shaped pools, providing tailored coverage and heat retention. Rectangle solar blankets are well-suited for traditional rectangular pools, offering comprehensive heat retention and protection from debris. Each type of solar blanket caters to different pool shapes and sizes, ensuring optimal energy efficiency and maintenance.</p></p>

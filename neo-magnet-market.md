<p><p>Neo magnets, also known as neodymium magnets, are commonly manufactured through two main processes: bonding and sintering. In the bonding market, magnets are created by mixing neodymium powder with a polymer binder, resulting in a flexible and versatile magnet that can be easily shaped and customized for various applications. On the other hand, in the sintering market, magnets are produced by compressing neodymium powder into a desired shape and then sintering it at high temperatures, resulting in a high-strength magnet with excellent magnetic properties.</p></p>

<p><p>Piezo inkjet print heads are categorized into two main types: MEMS inkjet printheads and non-MEMS inkjet printheads. MEMS inkjet printheads are based on microelectromechanical systems technology, which allows for precise control over ink droplets. Non-MEMS inkjet printheads, on the other hand, use different technologies for droplet ejection. Both types cater to different market segments, with MEMS printheads usually targeted towards industrial and commercial applications requiring high-quality prints, while non-MEMS printheads are more commonly used in consumer-level printers.</p></p>

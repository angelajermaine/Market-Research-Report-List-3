<p><ul><li>Metal Based Devices</li><li>Semiconductor Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spintronics technology is divided into two main market types: metal-based devices and semiconductor devices. Metal-based devices utilize magnetic metals to control the spin of electrons, while semiconductor devices use materials like gallium arsenide to achieve the same effect. Metal-based devices offer higher efficiency and lower power consumption, making them suitable for applications like magnetic sensors. On the other hand, semiconductor devices are more widely used in memory devices and logic circuits due to their compatibility with existing semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>

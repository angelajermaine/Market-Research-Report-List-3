<p><ul><li>Central Systems</li><li>Local Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial air conditioning systems can be categorized into two main types: central systems and local systems. Central systems have a centralized unit that cools the air and distributes it throughout an entire building. This type of system is commonly used in large industrial spaces. Local systems, on the other hand, consist of individual units that are installed in specific areas or rooms within a building. These systems are more flexible and can be easily controlled to meet the cooling needs of each specific area.</p></p>

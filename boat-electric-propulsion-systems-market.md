<p><strong>The Boat Electric Propulsion Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shaftline Propulsion</li><li>Pod Propulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boat electric propulsion systems come in two main types: shaftline propulsion and pod propulsion. Shaftline propulsion involves an electric motor connected to a propeller shaft which drives the boat forward. On the other hand, pod propulsion includes a self-contained unit with an electric motor and propeller that can be rotated for steering. Both systems offer efficient and environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional diesel engines, with pod propulsion being popular for its maneuverability and ease of installation.</p></p>

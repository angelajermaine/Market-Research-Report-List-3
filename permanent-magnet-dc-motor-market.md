<p><ul><li>Slotted Type</li><li>Slotless Type</li><li>Coreless Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Permanent Magnet DC motors are categorized into three main types based on their design and construction - Slotted Type, Slotless Type, and Coreless Type. Slotted Type motors have iron slots in the stator which help improve efficiency and torque. Slotless Type motors eliminate the iron slots, reducing eddy current losses and enabling faster acceleration. Coreless Type motors have no iron core, making them lightweight and ideal for applications requiring high acceleration and response speeds. Each type has its own unique advantages and is suited for different applications.</p></p>

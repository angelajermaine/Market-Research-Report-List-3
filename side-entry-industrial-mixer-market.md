Paddle mixers use rotating blades to mix materials, while turbine mixers utilize high-speed impellers. Magnetic mixers rely on magnetic coupling to drive the mixing process without direct contact between the motor and the mixing chamber. Other types of side-entry industrial mixers may include propeller mixers or ribbon mixers, each suited for specific applications and industries. These different mixer types offer a range of options for manufacturers to choose from based on their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>

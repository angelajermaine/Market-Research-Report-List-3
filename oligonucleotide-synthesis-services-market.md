<p><p>Oligonucleotide synthesis services market includes various types such as Primers, Probes, Intermediate-scale synthesis oligos, Large-scale synthesis oligos, Linkers, and Adaptors. Primers are short DNA fragments used as starting points for DNA synthesis. Probes are used to detect specific sequences in DNA or RNA. Intermediate-scale synthesis oligos are medium-sized oligos used for research purposes. Large-scale synthesis oligos are used for commercial applications. Linkers and adaptors are used to facilitate DNA or RNA manipulation in molecular biology experiments.</p></p>

<p><p>The Off-highway Diesel Common Rail Injection System market includes two main types of injectors - Conventional Solenoid Injector and Piezoelectric Injector. Conventional Solenoid Injectors use a solenoid to open and close the injector, controlling the fuel flow. On the other hand, Piezoelectric Injectors use a piezoelectric crystal to control the fuel flow with greater precision and speed. Both types of injectors play a crucial role in optimizing fuel consumption and improving engine performance in off-highway vehicles.</p></p>

<p><p>Multiport plug valves are valves with multiple ports or openings for flow control. They come in three main types: manual, pneumatic, and electric. Manual multiport plug valves are operated by hand, pneumatic ones use compressed air to actuate the valve, and electric versions use electrical power for operation. Each type offers its own advantages depending on the application, with manual valves being simple and cost-effective, pneumatic valves providing quicker response times, and electric valves offering precise control and automation capabilities.</p></p>

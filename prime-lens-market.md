<p><p>Prime lenses are classified into three main types based on their focal lengths: Normal lenses, Wide-angle lenses, and Telephoto lenses. Normal lenses have a focal length similar to the human eye, providing a natural field of view. Wide-angle lenses have shorter focal lengths, allowing for a wider field of view, making them ideal for landscape and architectural photography. Telephoto lenses have longer focal lengths, allowing for closer shots of distant subjects, making them popular for wildlife and sports photography. Each type caters to different photographic needs and styles.</p></p>

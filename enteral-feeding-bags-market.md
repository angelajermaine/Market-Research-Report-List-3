<p><p>Enteral feeding bags are used for administering nutrition directly into the digestive tract. There are two main types in the market: pump type and gravity type. Pump type enteral feeding bags use a pump to regulate the flow rate of the feeding, allowing for precise control. Gravity type enteral feeding bags rely on gravity to deliver the formula at a set flow rate. Both types have their own advantages and are used based on the patient's condition and specific needs.</p></p>

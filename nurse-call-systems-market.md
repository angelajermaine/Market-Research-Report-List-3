<p><strong>The Nurse Call Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wired Nurse Call Systems</li><li>Wireless Nurse Call Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nurse call systems are categorized into two main types: wired nurse call systems and wireless nurse call systems. Wired nurse call systems involve physical connections between the call buttons and the central monitoring unit, making them more reliable but potentially more costly to install. On the other hand, wireless nurse call systems use radio frequency technology to transmit signals between the call buttons and the central monitoring unit, offering greater flexibility and easier installation. Both types of systems play a crucial role in improving communication and response times in healthcare facilities.</p></p>

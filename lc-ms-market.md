<p><p>LC-MS market includes various types such as Single Quadrupole LC-MS, Triple Quadrupole LC-MS, Ion Trap LC-MS, and Others. Ion Trap LC-MS allows for fragmentation and isolation of ions for structural elucidation. Other types of LC-MS may include Time-of-Flight (TOF) and Quadrupole-Time-of-Flight (Q-TOF) instruments, which offer high resolution and accurate mass measurements for various applications.</p></p>

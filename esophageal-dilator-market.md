<p><p>Esophageal dilators are devices used to widen the esophagus in cases of narrowing or blockage. There are two main types of esophageal dilators on the market: mechanical esophageal dilators and balloon esophageal dilators. Mechanical esophageal dilators are rigid instruments that are inserted into the esophagus to gently widen the passageway. Balloon esophageal dilators, on the other hand, are inflatable devices that are inserted into the esophagus and then inflated to widen the narrowed area. Both types of dilators are used in medical procedures to treat conditions such as strictures or dysphagia.</p></p>

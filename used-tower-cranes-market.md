Flat Top Tower Cranes have a flat top without a crane cabin, suitable for constrained working areas. Hammerhead Tower Cranes have a fixed horizontal jib and a vertical mast for heavy lifting. Luffing Jib Tower Cranes have a flexible jib that can move up and down, ideal for crowded urban construction sites. Each type serves a specific purpose in the construction industry.</p></p>

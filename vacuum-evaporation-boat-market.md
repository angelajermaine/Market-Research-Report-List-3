<p><p>Vacuum evaporation boats are available in three main types: special ceramics based, metal based, and other based. Special ceramics based boats are made from materials like aluminum oxide or boron nitride, offering high temperature resistance and chemical inertness. Metal based boats are typically made from materials like tungsten or molybdenum, providing durability and stability at high temperatures. Other based boats may be made from various specialized materials tailored for specific applications, offering unique properties such as high thermal conductivity or enhanced corrosion resistance.</p></p>

<p><p>Spindle ball bearings are commonly categorized into various types based on their design and function. Angular-contact ball bearings are specifically designed to handle combined radial and axial loads. Radial ball bearings are primarily used to support radial loads. Roller bearings utilize cylindrical, tapered, or spherical rolling elements for handling heavy radial and axial loads. Thrust bearings are designed to support axial loads in one direction. Other types of spindle ball bearings include self-aligning ball bearings, precision bearings, and miniature bearings, each serving specific applications in various industries.</p></p>

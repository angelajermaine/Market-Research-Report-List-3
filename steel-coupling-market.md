<p><ul><li>Rigid Coupling</li><li>Flexible Coupling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel Coupling Market Types include Rigid Coupling and Flexible Coupling. Rigid Couplings are solid, stiff couplings that provide a direct connection between two shafts, ensuring precise alignment and high torque transmission. On the other hand, Flexible Couplings are designed to accommodate misalignment and reduce shock and vibration between shafts. They are ideal for applications where slight misalignment or movement is expected. Both types of couplings play a crucial role in various industrial applications for connecting shafts and transmitting power efficiently.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Doping or Ion Implantation</li><li>Etching</li><li>Deposition of Various Materials</li><li>Photolithographic Patterning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single wafer processing systems are specialized equipment used in semiconductor manufacturing for various purposes. These systems can be categorized into different types based on their functions, such as doping or ion implantation, etching, deposition of various materials, and photolithographic patterning. Doping or ion implantation systems are used to introduce impurities into the wafer to alter its electrical properties. Etching systems are used to remove layers of material from the wafer. Deposition systems are used to deposit thin films of various materials onto the wafer. Photolithographic patterning systems are used to transfer a pattern onto the wafer for further processing.</p></p>

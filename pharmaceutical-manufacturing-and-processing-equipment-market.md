<p><p>Pharmaceutical manufacturing and processing equipment consists of two main types – API equipment and finished product processing equipment markets. API equipment is used in the production of active pharmaceutical ingredients, while finished product processing equipment is used in the manufacturing of final drugs. API equipment typically includes reactors, filters, and dryers, while finished product processing equipment includes mixers, tablet presses, and packaging machines. Both markets play a crucial role in the pharmaceutical industry by ensuring the quality and efficiency of drug production.</p></p>

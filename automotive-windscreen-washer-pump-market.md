<p><p>The Automotive Windscreen Washer Pump market consists of two main types: Mono Pump and Dual Pump. Mono Pump systems have a single pump that delivers fluid to both the front and rear windscreens. On the other hand, Dual Pump systems have two separate pumps, one for the front windscreen and one for the rear. Mono pumps are generally simpler and more cost-effective, while dual pump systems provide better performance and efficiency by allowing independent control of fluid delivery to each windscreen.</p></p>

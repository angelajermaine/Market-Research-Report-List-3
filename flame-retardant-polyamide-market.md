<p><p>Flame Retardant Polyamide Market Types include Halogen Type and Halogen Free Type. Halogen Type flame retardants typically contain bromine or chlorine compounds that are effective in reducing flammability. However, they are facing increasing scrutiny due to environmental and health concerns. On the other hand, Halogen Free Type flame retardants use phosphorus, nitrogen, or other alternative compounds to achieve flame retardancy without the use of halogens. They are gaining popularity as a more sustainable and environmentally friendly option in the market.</p></p>

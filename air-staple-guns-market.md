<p><p>Air staple guns are tools used for fastening materials together using staples. They are commonly used in construction, woodworking, upholstery, and other applications where a secure bond is required. Air staple guns operate by compressing air in a chamber and releasing it to drive a staple into the material. They offer quick and efficient stapling, making them popular among professionals and DIY enthusiasts.</p><p>The Air Staple Guns Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.1% during the forecast period.

<p><p>Attention Deficit and Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) market types include stimulant and non-stimulant medications. Stimulant medications, such as Adderall and Ritalin, are commonly prescribed to manage ADHD symptoms by increasing dopamine and norepinephrine levels in the brain. Non-stimulant medications, like Strattera and Intuniv, work differently by targeting neurotransmitters to improve focus and control impulsivity. Both stimulant and non-stimulant medications are used to treat ADHD, but they have distinct mechanisms of action and may be prescribed based on individual needs and preferences.</p></p>

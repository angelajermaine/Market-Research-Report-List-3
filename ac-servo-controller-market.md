<p><p>AC Servo Controller market can be classified into two main types based on axis control: Uniaxial and Multiaxial. Uniaxial systems control motion in only one axis, making them suitable for simple linear applications. On the other hand, Multiaxial systems have the capability to control multiple axes simultaneously, making them ideal for complex motion control tasks such as robotics and CNC machines. Both types offer different levels of precision, speed, and flexibility to meet the diverse needs of various industries.</p></p>

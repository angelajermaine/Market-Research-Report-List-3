<p><p>Heavy Duty Industrial pH Sensors come in two main types: Glass sensors and ISFET sensors. Glass sensors are traditional and widely-used in industrial settings, known for their accuracy and durability. On the other hand, ISFET sensors are newer technology, utilizing a solid-state ion-sensitive field-effect transistor to measure pH levels. ISFET sensors are known for their faster response times, miniaturization, and resistance to clogging, making them popular in industries where quick and accurate pH measurements are crucial.</p></p>

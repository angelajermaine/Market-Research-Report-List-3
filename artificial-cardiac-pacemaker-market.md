<p><p>The Artificial Cardiac Pacemaker Market includes three main types of devices: Pacemakers, Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs), and Biventricular ICDs (BI-V ICDs). Pacemakers are used to regulate the heart rate, while ICDs are designed to deliver a shock to the heart in case of life-threatening arrhythmias. BI-V ICDs are a combination of both technologies, providing therapy for both heart rate regulation and arrhythmia management. The ICD market is a significant segment within the Artificial Cardiac Pacemaker Market, catering to patients with high-risk arrhythmias.</p></p>

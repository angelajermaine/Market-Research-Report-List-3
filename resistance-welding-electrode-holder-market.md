<p><p>Resistance welding electrode holder market comes in two main types: insulated and non-insulated. Insulated electrode holders are designed to protect users from electrical shocks by preventing current from passing through the holder. On the other hand, non-insulated electrode holders do not have this protective feature. Both types have their own advantages and applications based on the specific requirements of the welding process. Insulated holders are typically used for high-voltage applications, while non-insulated holders are more commonly used for lower voltage welding operations.</p></p>

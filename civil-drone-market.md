<p><p>The civil drone market can be divided into two main types: fixed-wing drones and rotary-wing drones. Fixed-wing drones are designed for longer flight times and are more efficient for covering large areas, making them ideal for applications such as aerial mapping and agriculture. On the other hand, rotary-wing drones, commonly known as quadcopters or drones with multiple rotors, are more versatile and can hover in place, making them suitable for tasks that require precise positioning or monitoring of vertical structures. Both types of drones play a crucial role in various industries, offering unique capabilities for different applications.</p></p>

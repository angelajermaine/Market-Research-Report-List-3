<p><p>I-joists are commonly used in the construction industry for their strength and durability. They come in various market types including Plywood, Oriented strand board (OSB), and Others. Plywood I-joists are made from layers of wood veneers glued together, providing a sturdy and stable option. OSB I-joists are composed of wood strands oriented in specific directions and pressed together, offering a cost-effective alternative. Other types of I-joists may include composite materials or metal components, catering to specific construction needs.</p></p>

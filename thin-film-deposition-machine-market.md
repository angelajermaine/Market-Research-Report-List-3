<p><p>Thin-film deposition machines are used for depositing thin films of various materials on surfaces for manufacturing electronic components and other applications. Three main types of deposition equipment are commonly used - PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) equipment, CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) equipment, and ALD (Atomic Layer Deposition) equipment. PVD equipment utilizes physical processes like evaporation or sputtering, CVD equipment involves chemical reactions, and ALD equipment involves a layer-by-layer deposition process. Each type of equipment offers specific advantages and is used for different applications in the thin-film industry.</p></p>

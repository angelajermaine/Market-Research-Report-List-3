<p><p>Electric Air Compressors are categorized into Single Stage and Two-Stage types based on their compression process. Single Stage Electric Air Compressors compress air in one stroke, suitable for light-duty applications. Two-Stage Electric Air Compressors compress air in two stages, which results in higher air pressure output, making them ideal for heavy-duty and industrial applications. Both types are widely used in various industries for tasks such as powering pneumatic tools, inflating tires, and supplying clean compressed air.</p></p>

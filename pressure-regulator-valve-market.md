<p><ul><li>Single-stage</li><li>Two-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure Regulator Valve market is categorized into single-stage and two-stage types. Single-stage regulators provide a constant outlet pressure regardless of changes in inlet pressure, making them suitable for applications where a stable pressure is required. On the other hand, two-stage regulators offer more precise control by reducing the inlet pressure in two steps, resulting in a more consistent outlet pressure. Both types cater to different requirements in various industries, with single-stage regulators offering simplicity and two-stage regulators offering precision.</p></p>

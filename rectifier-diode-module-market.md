<p><p>Rectifier Diode Module market types include Ordinary Rectifier Diode Module, which are commonly used for general purpose rectification applications. Fast Recovery Diode Module, known for its fast recovery time and low reverse recovery charge, is suitable for high-frequency switching applications. Schottky Diode Module, featuring low forward voltage drop and fast switching speed, is used in low voltage applications. Other types of diode modules may include Zener diodes, avalanche diodes, and light-emitting diodes, each designed for specific functions and applications.</p></p>

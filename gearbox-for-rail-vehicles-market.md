<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Drive</li><li>Transversal Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gearbox for rail vehicles market can be classified based on the drive arrangement, with two main types being longitudinal drive and transversal drive. Longitudinal drive systems have the gearbox and motor arranged parallel to the length of the vehicle, providing efficient power transfer. On the other hand, transversal drive systems have the gearbox and motor arranged perpendicular to the vehicle's length, offering better weight distribution and traction. Both market types cater to specific requirements and preferences of rail vehicle manufacturers and operators.</p></p>

<p><p>Hemodialysis access catheters are used for temporary or long-term dialysis treatment. Short-term catheters are temporary solutions inserted for immediate use, usually lasting a few days to weeks. Long-term catheters are used for prolonged dialysis treatment, typically lasting several months to years. Both types of catheters provide access to the bloodstream for hemodialysis, but long-term catheters are designed to remain in the body for extended periods, reducing the need for frequent catheter changes.</p></p>

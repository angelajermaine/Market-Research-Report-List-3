<p><strong>The Thermal Management System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal management systems can be categorized into two types: passive and active. Passive systems rely on natural heat transfer mechanisms such as conduction, convection, and radiation to dissipate heat without the need for additional energy input. Active systems, on the other hand, use mechanical components such as fans, pumps, and refrigeration units to actively remove heat from a system. Both types are commonly used in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and aerospace to prevent overheating and maintain optimal operating temperatures.</p></p>

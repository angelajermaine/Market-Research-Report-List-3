<p><p>Folding blade agitators are classified into different types based on their entry points into the mixing vessel. Top-entry agitators are installed from the top of the tank and are suitable for mixing applications where the liquid level remains constant. Side-entry agitators are mounted on the side of the vessel for applications where thorough mixing is required. Bottom-entry agitators are ideal for heavy-duty mixing tasks. Other types of folding blade agitators include portable and magnetic agitators, each designed for specific mixing requirements.</p></p>

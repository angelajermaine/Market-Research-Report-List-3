<p><p>Automatic Ultrasonic Welders are classified into two main types: Fully Automatic Ultrasonic Welders and Semi-Automatic Ultrasonic Welders. Fully automatic welders are capable of performing the welding process from start to finish without any manual intervention, making them ideal for high-volume production. On the other hand, semi-automatic welders require some level of human input to operate, such as loading parts or initiating the welding process. Both types offer efficiency and precision in welding operations, catering to different production requirements.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Automotive Independent Front Suspension System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double-transverse Wishbone Suspension System</li><li>MacPherson Suspension System</li><li>Short Swinging Arm System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive independent front suspension system market consists of three main types: the double-transverse wishbone suspension system, the MacPherson suspension system, and the short swinging arm system. The double-transverse wishbone suspension system uses two wishbone-shaped control arms to allow for a greater range of motion. The MacPherson suspension system integrates the shock absorber into the suspension strut, simplifying design and reducing overall weight. The short swinging arm system utilizes a compact design, making it ideal for smaller vehicles with limited space in the front suspension area.</p></p>

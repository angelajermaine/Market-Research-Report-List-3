<p><p>A cold roll laminator is a machine used to apply a protective film to documents, photos, posters, and other printed materials without the need for heat. The process involves feeding the material through rollers where pressure is applied to bond the film to the substrate. Cold roll laminators are popular for businesses, schools, and print shops due to their ease of use and versatility in laminating various sizes and types of materials.</p><p>The cold roll laminator market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.7% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for protection and preservation of printed materials, along with the rising trend of customization and personalization in the printing industry, are driving the growth of the cold roll laminator market. Additionally, advancements in technology, such as the development of automatic and digital cold roll laminators, are also contributing to market growth. The market is also witnessing a trend towards eco-friendly laminating solutions, with manufacturers introducing solvent-free and recyclable laminating films to meet the growing demand for sustainable printing practices.</p></p>

<p><p>Security radar sensors come in two main types: Imaging radar sensors and Non-imaging radar sensors. Imaging radar sensors use advanced technology to create high-resolution images of the surrounding area, allowing for detailed analysis and tracking of objects. Non-imaging radar sensors, on the other hand, do not produce images but instead focus on detecting and monitoring movement and obstacles in their range. Both types play a crucial role in security systems, providing different capabilities for surveillance and threat detection.</p></p>

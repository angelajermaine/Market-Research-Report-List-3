<p><p>Single Mechanical Seals market includes different types such as Pusher Type Seals and Non-pusher Seals. Pusher Type Seals utilize a dynamic secondary sealing element that maintains contact with the rotating shaft. These are suitable for high-speed applications. Non-pusher Seals, on the other hand, do not have a dynamic secondary sealing element and rely on a stationary seal face. These are commonly used in applications with low to moderate speeds and pressures. Both types offer unique advantages based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>

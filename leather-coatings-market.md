<p><strong>The Leather Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne</li><li>Waterborne</li><li>UV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Leather coatings come in three main types based on their formulation: Solventborne, Waterborne, and UV coatings. Solventborne coatings contain volatile organic compounds and require proper ventilation during application. Waterborne coatings are eco-friendly as they contain lower levels of VOCs. UV coatings are cured instantly under ultraviolet light, resulting in faster production times. Each type of coating offers different benefits and properties, catering to various requirements in the leather industry. Customers can choose the most suitable option based on their specific needs.</p></p>

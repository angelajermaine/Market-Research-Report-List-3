<p><p>Compact utility loaders can be categorized into two main types: track and wheel loaders. Track loaders, also known as skid steer loaders, are equipped with tracks instead of wheels, giving them better traction and stability on uneven or soft terrain. Wheel loaders, as the name suggests, have wheels and are better suited for flat surfaces and maneuvering in tight spaces. Both types of loaders offer versatility and efficiency in various construction, landscaping, and agricultural applications.</p></p>

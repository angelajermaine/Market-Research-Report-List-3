<p><p>Utility microgrids come in three main types: AC microgrid systems, DC microgrid systems, and hybrid microgrid systems. AC microgrid systems use alternating current to distribute power, while DC microgrid systems use direct current for the same purpose. Hybrid microgrid systems combine both AC and DC systems to optimize energy distribution and storage. Each system has its own strengths and weaknesses, making it crucial for utility microgrid developers to choose the most suitable system based on their specific needs and goals.</p></p>

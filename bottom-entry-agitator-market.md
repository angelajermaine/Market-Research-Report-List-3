<p><p>Bottom-entry agitators are commonly utilized in various industries and come in different types. </p><p>1. Paddle agitators: These have blades that move in a radial direction, creating a vertical flow to mix substances efficiently. </p><p>2. Turbine agitators: These have curved blades that produce a combination of radial and axial flow, ideal for mixing viscous fluids.</p><p>3. Magnetic agitators: These use a magnetic coupling to rotate the impeller, eliminating the need for a mechanical seal and reducing the risk of contamination.</p><p>4. Other market: This category includes alternative agitator types such as propeller and helical agitators that cater to specific mixing requirements in different industries.</p></p>

HEPA filters are capable of removing 99.97% of particles as small as 0.3 microns, while ULPA filters can remove particles as small as 0.12 microns. Both types help ensure a high level of cleanliness in cleanroom environments by capturing particles and preventing them from entering the controlled area.</p></p>

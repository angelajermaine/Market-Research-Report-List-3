<p><strong>The LED Array Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard-Density Integrated Arrays</li><li>High-Density Integrated Arrays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Standard-density integrated arrays refer to LED arrays with a lower number of diodes per unit area, typically used for general lighting applications where light output is not a critical factor. On the other hand, high-density integrated arrays have a higher number of diodes per unit area, providing increased brightness and better color mixing capabilities. These arrays are commonly used in applications requiring high-resolution displays, such as large outdoor screens or digital signage. The choice between standard-density and high-density arrays depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>

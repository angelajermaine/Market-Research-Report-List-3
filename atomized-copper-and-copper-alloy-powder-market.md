<p><p>Atomized Copper and Copper Alloy Powder market can be classified into two main types: Water Atomized and Air Atomized. Water Atomized powder is produced by spraying molten copper or copper alloy into a water stream, resulting in fine spherical particles with high purity. On the other hand, Air Atomized powder is produced by spraying molten metal into a high-velocity gas stream, which produces irregularly shaped particles with lower purity compared to water atomized powder. Both types have unique characteristics and are used in various industries for different applications.</p></p>

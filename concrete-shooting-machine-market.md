<p><p>Concrete shooting machines are used for spraying concrete onto surfaces for construction purposes. There are two main types of concrete shooting markets - wet shooting and dry shooting. Wet shooting involves mixing the concrete with water before spraying it onto surfaces, whereas dry shooting uses pre-mixed dry concrete. Wet shooting is preferred for its ease of use and faster drying time, while dry shooting is popular for its lower water content and reduced rebound. Both markets cater to different construction needs and preferences.</p></p>

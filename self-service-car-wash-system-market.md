<p><p>Self-service car wash systems can be divided into two main types: manual car wash systems and automatic car wash systems. Manual car wash systems require the customer to physically scrub and rinse the vehicle themselves using provided equipment and products. Automatic car wash systems, on the other hand, use machine-operated brushes and jets to clean the vehicle without the need for customer involvement. Both systems cater to different customer preferences and offer convenience and efficiency in keeping vehicles clean and maintained.</p></p>

<p><p>Cervical orthoses market includes dynamic and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to allow controlled movement of the cervical spine while providing support and stabilization. These orthoses are commonly used in cases of acute injuries or conditions that require flexibility. Static orthotics, on the other hand, are rigid devices that immobilize the cervical spine to promote healing and prevent further injury. They are typically used in cases of severe trauma or instability. Both types of orthotics play a crucial role in managing various cervical spine conditions.</p></p>

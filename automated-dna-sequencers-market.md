<p><p>Automated DNA sequencers come in two main types: Chain Terminator DNA Sequencing and Next Generation DNA Sequencing. Chain Terminator DNA Sequencing involves using modified nucleotides that terminate DNA synthesis, allowing for the determination of DNA sequences. Next Generation DNA Sequencing, on the other hand, refers to a newer and more advanced method that allows for faster and more efficient sequencing of DNA, enabling researchers to analyze large amounts of genetic information in a shorter period of time.</p></p>

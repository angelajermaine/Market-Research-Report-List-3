<p><ul><li>Closed-cell Foam</li><li>Open-cell Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foam molding market is divided into two main types: closed-cell foam and open-cell foam. Closed-cell foam has sealed, tight cells that do not allow air or liquid to pass through, making it ideal for applications requiring insulation or flotation. Open-cell foam, on the other hand, has interconnected cells that allow air and liquid to pass through, providing cushioning and flexibility. Both types have unique advantages and are used in various industries such as construction, automotive, and packaging.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Depletion Mode</li><li>Enhancement Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GaN FETs can be categorized into two main types based on their operation modes: Depletion Mode and Enhancement Mode. Depletion Mode GaN FETs are normally on without a bias voltage applied, while Enhancement Mode GaN FETs require a positive gate voltage to turn on. Depletion Mode FETs are typically used in high-power applications where efficiency is crucial, while Enhancement Mode FETs are more commonly found in low-power applications where fast switching speeds are required. Both types have their specific market niches based on their unique characteristics and advantages.</p></p>

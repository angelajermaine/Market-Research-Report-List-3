<p><p>Automotive Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) system is an emission control technology used in diesel engines to reduce harmful nitrogen oxide (NOx) emissions. The system uses a liquid reductant, typically urea, which is injected into the exhaust stream where it reacts with the NOx to form harmless nitrogen and water vapor. SCR systems have become increasingly popular in the automotive industry as they provide a more efficient way to meet stringent emission regulations.</p><p>The Automotive Selective Catalytic Reduction System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.7% during the forecast period.

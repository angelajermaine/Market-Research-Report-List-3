<p><p>Network Surveillance Cameras market can be divided into two main types - Centralized IP Cameras and Decentralized IP Cameras. Centralized IP Cameras require a central network video recorder (NVR) to manage and store footage, offering centralized control over multiple cameras. On the other hand, Decentralized IP Cameras have built-in storage capabilities, recording footage directly onto the camera itself without the need for a central NVR. Both types offer unique advantages and cater to different surveillance needs in the market.</p></p>

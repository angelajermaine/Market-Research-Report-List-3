<p><p>Custom hearing aids refer to devices that are specifically molded to fit the wearer's ear canal or outer ear. Both types provide a customized fit for improved comfort and sound quality. These types of hearing aids are popular choices for individuals seeking discreet and personalized hearing solutions.</p></p>

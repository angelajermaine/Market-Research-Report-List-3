<p><p>Liquid Nitrogen Traps are used to capture and remove impurities in gas streams. There are two main types of liquid nitrogen traps available in the market: Inline and Right Angle traps. Inline traps are installed directly in the gas line, allowing for easy integration and maintenance. On the other hand, Right Angle traps are positioned at a right angle to the gas flow, providing efficient trapping of contaminants. Both types of traps are essential in various industries for ensuring the purity of gas streams.</p></p>

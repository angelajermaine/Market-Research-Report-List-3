<p><strong>The Bio-based Renewable Jet Fuel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HEFA-SPK</li><li>FT-SPK</li><li>ATJ-SPK</li><li>Co-processing</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bio-based renewable jet fuel market types include HEFA-SPK (Hydroprocessed Esters and Fatty Acids - Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene), FT-SPK (Fischer-Tropsch Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene), ATJ-SPK (Alcohol-to-Jet Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene), Co-processing, and others. HEFA-SPK is produced from vegetable oils or animal fats through hydroprocessing. FT-SPK is produced from gas-to-liquid technology. ATJ-SPK is produced from alcohols. Co-processing involves blending bio-feedstocks with petroleum feedstocks. Other types include renewable hydroprocessed esters and fatty acids (HEFA) and renewable isoparaffins. Each type offers unique advantages and is gaining traction in the aviation industry.</p></p>

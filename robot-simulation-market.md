<p><p>Robot simulation market can be classified into two main types: cloud-based and local deployment. Cloud-based simulation allows users to access the software remotely, eliminating the need for hardware installation and maintenance. This provides flexibility and scalability for users. In contrast, local deployment involves installing the simulation software on a physical machine, offering more control and security. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of users, depending on factors such as cost, accessibility, and data security.</p></p>

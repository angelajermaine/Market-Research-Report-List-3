<p><strong>The Induction Tachogenerators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cage Rotor</li><li>Hollow Cup Rotor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Induction tachogenerators are used to measure the speed of rotating machinery. Two common types in the market are the cage rotor and hollow cup rotor. The cage rotor consists of a squirrel cage winding embedded in the rotor, which induces a current when the rotor rotates. The hollow cup rotor has a cup-shaped rotor with coils wound around the inner surface, producing a voltage as it spins. Both types are widely used in industries for monitoring and controlling the speed of machines.</p></p>

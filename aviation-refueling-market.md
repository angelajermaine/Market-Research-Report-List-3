<p><p>The aviation refueling market consists of various types of equipment used for supplying fuel to aircraft. Mobile tankers are trucks equipped with fuel storage tanks that can transport fuel directly to aircraft for refueling. Hydrant dispensers are stationary units connected to underground pipelines that deliver fuel to aircraft parked at gates. Fueling cabinets/skids are compact units installed at airfields for refueling operations. Each market type caters to different refueling needs in the aviation industry, offering efficiency and convenience for aircraft operations.</p></p>

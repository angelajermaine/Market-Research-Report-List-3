<p><p>Smart Electric Bidet Seats are available in three main types in the market - Tank Type, Tank-less Type, and Hybrid Type. Tank Type bidet seats store and heat water in a reservoir before use, providing a consistent temperature throughout. Tank-less Type bidet seats heat water on demand, offering instant hot water supply. Hybrid Type bidet seats combine the features of both tank and tank-less types by utilizing a small reservoir for immediate use and a continuous heating system for longer use. Each type offers unique features to cater to different preferences and needs.</p></p>

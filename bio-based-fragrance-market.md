<p><p>Bio-based fragrance market types refer to different categories of fragrances derived from natural sources. Tetracyclic biological base aromatics are compounds with four interconnected rings that are commonly found in plants and flowers, giving off complex and exotic scents. Hexyclic biological base aromatics, on the other hand, are compounds with six-membered carbon rings that are often present in citrus fruits and provide a fresh and uplifting fragrance. These natural ingredients are increasingly popular in the fragrance industry due to their sustainable and eco-friendly characteristics.</p></p>

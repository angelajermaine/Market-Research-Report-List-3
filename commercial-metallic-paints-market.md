<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial metallic paints are available in two main types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based metallic paints contain volatile organic compounds and require specific safety measures during application. They offer excellent adhesion and durability, making them suitable for industrial applications. On the other hand, water-based metallic paints are environmentally friendly, with low levels of VOCs. They are easy to clean up and produce minimal odor during application. Water-based metallic paints are often preferred for interior applications due to their low toxicity and eco-friendly nature.</p></p>

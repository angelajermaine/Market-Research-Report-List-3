<p><p>Gastrointestinal videoscopes are medical devices used to examine the gastrointestinal tract. There are two main types of gastrointestinal videoscopes: hard mirror and flexible soft mirror. </p><p>Hard mirror videoscopes have a rigid structure with a fixed angle, allowing for direct visualization of the gastrointestinal tract. </p><p>Flexible soft mirror videoscopes have a bendable shaft that can navigate through the twists and turns of the gastrointestinal tract, providing a more comprehensive view of the inner lining. Both types of videoscopes play a crucial role in diagnosing and treating gastrointestinal conditions.</p></p>

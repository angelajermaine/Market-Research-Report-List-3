<p><p>The Aviation Tracking System market encompasses three main types: ADS-B (Automatic Dependent Surveillance-Broadcast), FANS (Future Air Navigation System), and PFTS (Position and Flight Tracking System). ADS-B uses satellite technology to track aircraft positions in real-time, providing accurate and reliable information for air traffic control. FANS is a communication system that enables direct communication between pilots and air traffic controllers, enhancing safety and efficiency. PFTS combines various tracking technologies to monitor aircraft positions and flight trajectories, ensuring optimal flight management and safety measures.</p></p>

<p><p>Food stuffer market is segmented into three main types: manual stuffer, automatic stuffer, and hydraulic stuffer. Manual stuffers require physical effort to operate and are suitable for smaller volume applications. Automatic stuffers are powered by electricity or compressed air and offer higher production capacity and efficiency. Hydraulic stuffers are the most powerful and efficient, using hydraulic power to push the food mixture into casings. Each type of stuffer caters to different needs and preferences of consumers in the food processing industry.</p></p>

<p><p>Body cleansers come in two main types: acidic and alkalic. Acidic body cleansers have a lower pH level, typically between 4-6, making them suitable for individuals with sensitive skin or conditions like eczema. On the other hand, alkalic body cleansers have a higher pH level, around 8-10, and are better at removing oil and dirt from the skin. Both types offer different benefits and cater to different skin types, so it is important to choose the right one based on individual needs.</p></p>

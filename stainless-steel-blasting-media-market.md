<p><p>Stainless Steel Shot Blasting Media is a type of abrasive material used for surface preparation and finishing of metal components. It consists of small, round pellets that are shot at high speeds onto the surface to remove rust, scale, and other contaminants. Stainless Steel Grit Blasting Media, on the other hand, consists of angular particles that are more aggressive and used for heavy-duty blasting applications. Both types of media are commonly used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for cleaning and deburring metal parts.</p></p>

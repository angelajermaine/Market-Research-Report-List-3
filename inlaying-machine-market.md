<p><strong>The Inlaying Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot-pressing Inlaying</li><li>Cold-pressing Inlaying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot-pressing inlaying machines involve the use of heat and pressure to bond materials together, typically used for woodworking and furniture making. Cold-pressing inlaying machines, on the other hand, use pressure alone without the need for heat, commonly used for delicate materials like veneers or inlay work on musical instruments. Both types of machines serve the purpose of securely embedding one material into another, but the method of application differs based on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>

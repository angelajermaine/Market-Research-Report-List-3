<p><strong>The Dry Gas Mechanical Seals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contacting Gas Seal</li><li>Non-Contacting Gas Seal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dry Gas Mechanical Seals are mainly categorized into Contacting Gas Seals and Non-Contacting Gas Seals based on their operation. Contacting Gas Seals use physical contact between seal faces to create a barrier against gas leakage, while Non-Contacting Gas Seals use non-contacting technology such as magnetic or aerodynamic forces to prevent gas leakage. Both types play a crucial role in various industries where preventing gas leakage is essential, such as oil and gas, chemical, and pharmaceutical industries.</p></p>

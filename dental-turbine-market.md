<p><ul><li>Inside Exhaust</li><li>Outside Exhaust</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dental Turbine market consists of two main types: Inside Exhaust and Outside Exhaust. Inside Exhaust turbines are designed with exhaust air flowing inside the head of the handpiece, which reduces noise and vibrations. On the other hand, Outside Exhaust turbines have the exhaust air flowing outside the handpiece head, resulting in a quieter operation and improved infection control. Both types offer different benefits and cater to different preferences among dental professionals.</p></p>

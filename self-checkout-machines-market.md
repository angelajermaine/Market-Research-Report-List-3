<p><p>Self-checkout machines come in two main types: semi-automatic and fully automatic. Semi-automatic machines require some level of manual intervention from the user, such as scanning items and inputting payment information. Fully automatic machines, on the other hand, are more advanced and require minimal to no intervention from the user as they can automatically scan items, process payments, and bag items. Both types of machines aim to streamline the checkout process, reduce wait times, and improve overall customer convenience in retail settings.</p></p>

<p><p>Surfactants are chemicals that are used in various industries for their surface-active properties. There are four main types of surfactants on the market: anionic surfactants, cationic surfactants, amphoteric surfactants, and nonionic surfactants. Anionic surfactants are negatively charged and are commonly used in household cleaning products. Cationic surfactants are positively charged and are often found in fabric softeners and hair conditioners. Amphoteric surfactants have both positive and negative charges, making them versatile for use in a wide range of applications. Nonionic surfactants do not have a charge and are often used in cosmetics and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>

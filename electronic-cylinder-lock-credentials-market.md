<p><p>Electronic cylinder lock credentials can be categorized into four main types: electronic key, RFID key, key fob, and badge. Electronic key, also known as contact-based key, requires physical contact with the lock to grant access. RFID key utilizes cards embedded with RFID chips for wireless access. Key fobs are small electronic devices that can be carried on a keychain for convenient access. Badges are typically used in commercial settings and feature RFID technology for secure access control.</p></p>

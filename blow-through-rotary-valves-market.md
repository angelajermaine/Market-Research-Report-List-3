<p><p>Blow-Through Rotary Valves are commonly used in material handling systems to control the flow of bulk solids. The three main types of Blow-Through Rotary Valves are Drop Thru Valves, Blow Thru Valves, and Side Entry Valves. Drop Thru Valves are suitable for gravity fed applications, Blow Thru Valves are ideal for pneumatic conveying systems, and Side Entry Valves are used for applications where space is limited. Each type offers unique features and benefits depending on the specific requirements of the material handling system.</p></p>

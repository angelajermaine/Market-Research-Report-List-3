<p><p>RF Microelectromechanical System (MEMS) market consists of various types such as RF capacitors and inductors, RF switches and derivatives, RF filters, and others. RF capacitors and inductors are used for storing and manipulating electrical energy in RF circuits. RF switches and derivatives allow for switching between different RF signals. RF filters help in selecting desired RF signals while rejecting unwanted signals. Other types in the market include components like resonators, oscillators, and phase shifters that are crucial for RF signal processing and manipulation.</p></p>

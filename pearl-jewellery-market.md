<p><p>Pearls are categorized into two main types: saltwater pearls and freshwater pearls. Saltwater pearls are typically larger, rounder, and more expensive, coming from oysters in oceans and seas. Freshwater pearls, on the other hand, are smaller, irregular in shape, and more affordable, harvested from mussels in rivers, lakes, and ponds. Both types of pearls are popular in the jewellery market, with saltwater pearls being more luxurious and suitable for high-end pieces, while freshwater pearls are more versatile and accessible for everyday wear.</p></p>

<p><p>IV dressings are available in two main types - transparent and translucent. Transparent IV dressings are made of clear, see-through material which allows healthcare providers to easily monitor the insertion site without removing the dressing. On the other hand, translucent IV dressings are slightly opaque, providing some level of privacy while still allowing for observation of the IV site. Both types offer protection against contamination and help maintain a sterile environment for IV therapy.</p></p>

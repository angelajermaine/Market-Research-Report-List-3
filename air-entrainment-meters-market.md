<p><p>Air Entrainment Meters are devices used to measure the amount of air entrained in concrete during the mixing process. There are two main types of Air Entrainment Meters: Water Column Type and Pressure Gauge Type. Water Column Type meters measure the amount of air in the concrete by displacing water in a column, while Pressure Gauge Type meters measure air content by monitoring changes in pressure within a sealed chamber. Both types of meters are essential tools for ensuring the proper air content in concrete for durability and workability.</p></p>

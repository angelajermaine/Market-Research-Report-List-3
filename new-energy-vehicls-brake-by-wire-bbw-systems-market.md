<p><p>The Brake By Wire (BBW) Systems market can be categorized into Two-Box and One-Box systems. Two-Box systems separate the hydraulic brake control unit from the electronic control unit, allowing for greater flexibility and customization. On the other hand, One-Box systems integrate both units into a single compact system, simplifying installation and reducing overall space requirements. Both systems offer their own advantages and are used in various types of new energy vehicles for efficient and reliable braking performance.</p></p>
